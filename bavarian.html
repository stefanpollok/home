Obatzda

Preparation: 2 min
Total time: 15 min

  1. Combine Camembert, cream cheese and butter in a large bowl by pressing it with a fork against the bowl wall.
    HINT: The warmer Camembert and butter are, the less workout it is.
  2. Slowly add salt, pepper, and paprika until the Obatzda obtains its light orange colour.
  3. Stir in the chopped onion.
  4. Cool the Obatzda for some hours in the fridge. During that time the Obatzda develops its special taste.
  5. Place chopped chive on top of the Obatzda before serving.

Serving sugesstion: Breze, Rugbrød
